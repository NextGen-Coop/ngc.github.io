NextGen Cooperative

Innovative Web and Mobile Solutions

WorkerBee specializes in web and mobile sites, but there's not much we can't do. Our developers are experts in their field and command a wide range of skills. Not only that, but we won't reinvent the wheel. We utilize free open-source technology to save us time and you money. Give us your idea, and together we can make it a reality.

Empowering Through Education

Hands-on learning and doing, mentored by seasoned professionals already in the industry. We will meet you where you are if you're willing to learn and want to offer assistance if you need equipment. Traditional boot camps and college courses are expensive and gatekeep tech education from low-income areas. We want to help remove those obstacles, and not just with our workers. We are planning on hosting computer literacy courses to teach our communities how to learn and teach themselves. Supporting us is supporting Middle Tennessee.

Community-Driven Success

WorkerBee is a worker cooperative, which is an ownership structure where our workers are our owners. No outside shareholders who wouldn't know what DNS is even if we spelled it out for them. No C-level executives pushing profits over people. Just our workers making sure everyone's voices are heard. Choosing WorkerBee to carry your project is a good choice; at every level, we want you to succeed because your success is our success.
