A cooperative business model functions similarly to traditional business models. The primary difference is that instead of being privately owned by a single entity or group, or publicly owned via stock shares purchasable on the market, we are entirely owned by the people who work here. Each member gets a vote on issues that affect the business, and the consensus wins. For more details, see our bylaws
